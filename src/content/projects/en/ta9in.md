A different kind of project manager. Most tools are designed for PMs managing teams.
This one is built for engineers who think in outcomes, not tasks.

The core idea: **context switching has a cost**. Everything in the tool is designed to minimize it —
group related work, surface what matters now, hide the rest.

Still in the concept phase. More details in the blog post.
